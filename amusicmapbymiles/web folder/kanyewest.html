Kanye West




Kanye West, born June 8, 1977, in Atlanta, Georgia, is a rapper, producer, and fashion designer who has had a profound impact on both music and popular culture. Rising to fame with his 2004 debut album The College Dropout, West is known for blending hip-hop with soulful, experimental production. His work spans various genres, pushing boundaries with albums like 808s & Heartbreak (2008) and My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy (2010). Beyond music, he is a fashion mogul with his Yeezy brand and has been involved in numerous public controversies, making him one of the most influential yet polarizing figures in modern entertainment.
