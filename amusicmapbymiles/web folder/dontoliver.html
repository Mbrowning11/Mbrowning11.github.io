Don Toliver




Don Toliver, born Caleb Zackery Toliver on June 12, 1994, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter from Houston, Texas. He gained widespread recognition with his feature on Travis Scott's track "Can't Say" in 2018. Known for his melodic vocals and trap-infused beats, Toliver released his debut studio album Heaven or Hell in 2020, which included hits like "No Idea" and "After Party." His follow-up album, Life of a DON (2021), further cemented his position as a leading voice in modern hip-hop, blending R&B, trap, and psychedelic sounds.
